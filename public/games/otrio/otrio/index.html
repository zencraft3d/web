Otrio

Otrio – Quick Rules #

Goal: Be the first to get three of your pieces in a row in the same size (small, medium, large), stacked (all sizes in one spot), or column/row/diagonal.

Setup: #

Each player chooses a color. Place the board in the center.

Gameplay: #

Players take turns placing one piece on the board. A piece can be placed: On an empty space On top of a smaller piece Pieces cannot go on top of a larger piece.

Winning: #

Form a row of three same size pieces. Form a row of three stacked pieces (small → medium → large). Form a stack of all three sizes in one space.

Notes: #

Only one winning combination is needed. Strategy comes from blocking opponents while building your combinations.
